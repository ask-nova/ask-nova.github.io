Mastering OTC Retail Excellence

Over-the-Counter pharmaceutical sales representatives face unique retail challenges focused on volume, visibility, and competitive positioning. Nova's AI companion transforms these challenges into market advantages.

Inventory Management & Stock Optimization

The Challenge:

Pharmacies struggle with optimal stock levels - too much inventory ties up capital, while too little leads to lost sales. Managing expiry dates, returns, and ensuring products are always visible is crucial for OTC success.

Nova's Solution:

  • Predictive Demand Forecasting: AI analyzes historical sales, seasonal trends, local events, and weather data to predict demand for specific OTC products at individual pharmacy locations
  • Automated Inventory Monitoring: Real-time stock level tracking with alerts for low stock or nearing-expiry products requiring immediate attention
  • Recommended Ordering: Optimized order suggestions to minimize stockouts and overstocking, directly benefiting sales performance

Merchandising & Shelf Space Optimization

The Challenge:

OTC products rely heavily on visibility and effective merchandising. Ensuring optimal shelf placement, planogram compliance, and up-to-date promotional materials in a fiercely competitive shelf space environment.

Nova's Solution:

  • Image Recognition for Planogram Compliance: AI analyzes shelf photos to check compliance, identify misplaced products, and highlight visibility opportunities
  • Optimized Merchandising Recommendations: Product performance-based suggestions for optimal placement, cross-merchandising opportunities, and display enhancements
  • Automated Audit & Reporting: Streamlined shelf presence auditing with automated report generation and compliance tracking

Competitive Intelligence & Market Dynamics

The Challenge:

The OTC market is highly dynamic with frequent new product launches, aggressive competitor promotions, and rapidly changing consumer preferences requiring real-time competitive intelligence.

Nova's Solution:

  • Competitive Monitoring & Alerts: Real-time scanning of competitor activities, pricing changes, promotions, and new product launches in your territory
  • Market Share Analysis: Pharmacy-level market share insights highlighting underperformance areas and growth opportunities
  • Consumer Trend Insights: Analysis of search trends, social media discussions, and sales data to inform emerging consumer demands

Personalized Pitches & Cross-selling

The Challenge:

Creating personalized conversations that address specific pharmacy customer bases, regional health trends, and business goals while identifying optimal cross-selling and upselling opportunities.

Nova's Solution:

  • Customer Segmentation & Profiling: AI segments pharmacies by size, location, demographics, sales history, and specific needs for tailored discussions
  • Product Recommendation Engine: Sales data-based suggestions for cross-selling opportunities and new product introductions
  • Talking Point Generator: Personalized sales narratives based on each pharmacy's unique situation and value proposition needs

High Call Volume & Territory Management

The Challenge:

OTC sales involve high-volume calls to numerous pharmacies within a territory. Optimizing routes, managing schedules, and ensuring consistent coverage presents significant logistical challenges.

Nova's Solution:

  • Dynamic Route Optimization: Highly efficient daily/weekly routes minimizing travel time and maximizing productive visits considering traffic and pharmacy hours
  • Visit Prioritization: AI-based pharmacy prioritization considering sales potential, last visit date, stock levels, and promotional cycles
  • Automated Task Management: Automatic generation of follow-up tasks, promotion reminders, and order placement calls

Administrative Efficiency & Reporting

The Challenge:

Valuable time spent on administrative tasks like order entry, visit reporting, expense management, and tracking promotional material usage reduces time for actual selling activities.

Nova's Solution:

  • Streamlined Order Entry: AI-assisted order creation based on stock recommendations and purchase history with system integration
  • Automated Call Reporting: Voice-to-text capture of visit details with pre-populated CRM reporting fields
  • Compliance Tracking: Automated checks for promotional materials and pricing discussions with regulatory adherence documentation
