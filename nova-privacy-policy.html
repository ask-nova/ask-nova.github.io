Ask-Nova™ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Date]

Introduction

NOVA AI Inc., a division of Valsoft Corporation Inc. ("NOVA AI," "we," "us," or "our"), is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the Ask-Nova™ AI companion platform (the "Platform" or "Service").

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through the Ask-Nova™ platform and any related services, sales, marketing, or events.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

Account and Registration Information:

  • Company name and business contact information
  • User names, email addresses, and authentication credentials
  • Job titles, roles, and department information
  • Billing and payment information (processed through secure third-party payment processors)

Customer Data:

  • CRM data you integrate with the Platform
  • Market data and competitive intelligence information
  • Digital engagement data and interaction histories
  • Healthcare professional information (names, specialties, contact information, professional affiliations)
  • Content you create, upload, or share through the Platform
  • Communications and queries you submit to the AI assistant

Information Collected Automatically

Usage Information:

  • Token consumption and conversation time
  • Platform features accessed and interaction patterns
  • Query types and conversation topics (anonymized)
  • Response times and system performance metrics
  • Device information, browser type, and operating system
  • IP addresses and geographic location data

Technical Information:

  • Log files and error reports
  • Mobile app usage data and crash reports
  • API calls and integration performance metrics

Information from Third Parties

Integrated Data Sources:

  • Data from your CRM system (Salesforce, Veeva, Microsoft Dynamics, etc.)
  • Market research and competitive intelligence data from authorized providers
  • Digital engagement data from marketing platforms
  • Healthcare professional data from licensed medical databases

How We Use Your Information

Primary Service Delivery

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Ask-Nova™ platform
  • Process your AI queries and generate intelligent responses
  • Integrate and synthesize data from multiple sources
  • Deliver predictive analytics and engagement recommendations
  • Provide real-time conversation support and contextual insights
  • Process voice commands and automate CRM updates

Platform Improvement and AI Development

We use information in aggregated and anonymized form to:

  • Improve AI model accuracy and response quality
  • Enhance machine learning algorithms and predictive capabilities
  • Develop new features and functionalities
  • Conduct research and development activities
  • Create industry benchmarks and comparative analyses
  • Analyze healthcare professional behavior patterns (anonymized and aggregated)

Important Limitation: Benchmark data and platform improvements never include customer-specific commercial data, engagement records, proprietary business information, or personally identifiable information.

Administrative Purposes

We use information to:

  • Manage user accounts and authentication
  • Process payments and maintain billing records
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Send service notifications and platform updates
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

Large Language Model Processing

The Ask-Nova™ platform currently utilizes Google's Gemini large language model to process queries and generate responses. When you interact with the Platform:

  • Your queries are processed by the LLM to generate intelligent responses
  • We may change LLM providers to enhance performance or reduce costs
  • LLM providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies
  • We implement technical safeguards to protect data shared with LLM providers
  • Personal data is minimized in LLM interactions where possible

Healthcare Professional Information

Collection and Processing

We may collect and process healthcare professional information, including:

  • Names, medical specialties, and professional credentials
  • Practice locations and institutional affiliations
  • Professional behavior patterns and communication preferences
  • Engagement trends across the pharmaceutical industry (anonymized)

Limitations and Protections

  • Healthcare professional data is collected solely for platform improvement and analytics
  • Individual healthcare professionals cannot be identified in benchmark data
  • No customer-specific commercial activities or engagement details are included
  • All processing complies with applicable healthcare privacy regulations
  • Data is aggregated and anonymized before use in platform improvements

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Information With:

Service Providers:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers (Microsoft Azure)
  • LLM providers (Google Gemini)
  • Payment processors and billing systems
  • Customer support and analytics tools

Authorized Resellers:

  • Your designated Ask-Nova™ reseller (e.g., Exeevo Inc.)
  • Only information necessary for sales, billing, and support purposes

Business Transfers:

  • In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • Successor entities will be bound by this Privacy Policy

Legal Requirements:

  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • To protect rights, property, or safety of NOVA AI, users, or others
  • To enforce our terms and conditions

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share customer-specific commercial or engagement data
  • Disclose individual healthcare professional activities to competitors
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to the Service without consent

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures including:

Technical Safeguards:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication for user accounts
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Access controls and role-based permissions

Organizational Safeguards:

  • Employee training on data protection and privacy
  • Confidentiality agreements with personnel and contractors
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures
  • Regular security audits and compliance reviews

Infrastructure Security:

  • Deployment within secure Microsoft Azure environments
  • Compliance with SOC 2, ISO 27001, and other security standards
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures

Data Retention

Customer Data: Retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days for data export

Account Information: Retained for 7 years after account closure for legal and compliance purposes

Usage Analytics: Retained in anonymized form indefinitely for platform improvement

Benchmark Data: Retained indefinitely in aggregated, anonymized form

Backup Data: Maintained according to standard backup retention policies (typically 90 days)

You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of benchmark data collection (may limit access to comparative analytics)
  • Restrict processing for specific purposes

Communication Preferences

You can control:

  • Marketing and promotional communications (opt-out available)
  • Service notifications and platform updates (required for service delivery)
  • Customer support communications

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@nova-ai.com or through your authorized reseller.

International Data Transfers

The Ask-Nova™ platform may process data in multiple jurisdictions, including:

  • Canada (primary operations)
  • United States (cloud infrastructure and LLM processing)
  • Other jurisdictions where Microsoft Azure services are deployed

We implement appropriate safeguards for international transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by data protection authorities
  • Adequacy decisions recognized by regulatory bodies
  • Technical and organizational security measures

Children's Privacy

The Ask-Nova™ platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected information from a child, we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:

  • Changes in our data practices
  • New features or services
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Feedback from users or regulators

We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notification to registered users
  • Providing in-app notifications

Your continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Regional Privacy Rights

For European Economic Area (EEA) and UK Residents

Under GDPR and UK Data Protection Act, you have additional rights including:

  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to data portability in machine-readable format
  • Right to lodge complaints with supervisory authorities
  • Right to withdraw consent for specific processing activities

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Contract performance for service delivery
  • Legitimate interests for platform improvement and analytics
  • Legal obligations for compliance and security
  • Consent where specifically obtained

For California Residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have rights including:

  • Right to know what information is collected and how it's used
  • Right to delete personal information (subject to exceptions)
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

For Canadian Residents

Under PIPEDA and provincial privacy laws, you have rights including:

  • Right to access personal information
  • Right to challenge accuracy of information
  • Right to withdraw consent (subject to legal/contractual restrictions)
  • Right to file complaints with Privacy Commissioner
